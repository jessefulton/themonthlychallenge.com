Sat, 06/28/2008 - 22:00 | by Jesse

That's right - I made it the full 10 miles today. And I'm also going to brag about it. I did the first 6 miles in about 45-50 minutes, and then the last 4 in about the same amount of time. I'm not too upset about that, it's a damn long run. At the beginning of the month, I was dead tired after a 3 mile run, but after the run today, my lungs and energy level felt pretty good. My muscles and joints on the other hand were a different story. About an hour after I got home, I could barely walk.

But, that didn't stop me from going to play paintball this afternoon (in ~100 degree heat.) I could barely walk, which I'm assuming is why I got hit so much. I got a few good head shots off, but then suffered a few shots right in my neck. And then I jacked up my knee jumping off of a dirt wall - it's hard to land a jump when you're holding a giant paintball gun and can't see your feet due to your face mask. Needless to say, I left the paintball field in much worse shape than I came in.

I had a hard time walking to my apartment from my car, so now I'm icing my knees and trying to find a restaurant that will deliver me delicious food and beverages, and possibly giving me a full-body massage.
