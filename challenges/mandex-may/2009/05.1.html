Challenge Date: 
May, 2009

Mandex is my new term for the manly spandex that I will be wearing as I bicycle around Los Angeles. All spandex becomes manly as soon as I put it on, so I don't own any spandex - only mandex. Why will I be cycling around LA? Well, at the end of May, I head up to San Francisco, only to begin a 575 mile bike ride back to Los Angeles as part of AIDS/LifeCycle 8. I've been training a bit, and felt pretty good up until a few weeks ago when I hurt my back. After almost two weeks of very little training, I feel like I'm in terrible shape, so this month I'm kicking it into high gear.

I will try to ride my bicycle every day (one rest day will probably consist of a light bike right to work) and try to meet the following weekly mileage.

  • May 1-7: 275 miles
  • May 8-14: 325 miles 300 miles
  • May 15-21: 375 miles 350 miles
  • May 22-28: 425 miles 315 miles

Mileages updated to be more realistic and account for holidays, bike shipments, work, etc.

The 29th & 30th will be my last chance to rest up for the big ride which (fittingly) begins on May 31st.

I'm going to try to commute by bike as much as I can, so don't be suprised if you invite me somewhere and I show up slightly sweaty and wearing my mandex. And by suprised, I mean aroused by my amazingly awesome manliness.
