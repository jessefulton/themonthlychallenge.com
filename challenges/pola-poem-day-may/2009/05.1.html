Challenge Date: 
May, 2009

this is a pretty straightforward challenge. each day in may i'll be taking a polaroid and then coming up with a little haiku to write on it. the photo can be of anything- what's happening to me that day, who i'm with, something i see that i like, or whatever. the haiku is going to loosely be about the polaroid image- either directly about it, about the reason i took that picture, or about how i'm feeling. if you read a haiku and think it has nothing to do with the image, that'll just be because you're not inside my head.

this should be a great month for this. this month i have to pack up my current apartment, and then move into my new bungalow. my mom, aunt, and cousin are visiting mid-month, and i've taken some vacation to spend time with them. 

looks like i'll have to switch to a bigger purse for the month so i can bring my camera everywhere.
