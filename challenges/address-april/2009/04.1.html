Challenge Date: 
April, 2009

So for my first monthly challenge I'm going to make and write thirty cards for thirty people in thirty days. Thirty.

Since I'm from the east coast I have a lot of friends and family that I never see. Email and Facebook are alright, you stay up to date, you can say "hi", but it clearly is not the same as getting a note in the mail. When I was working at the "Paper Store" (as everyone took to calling it) I became obsessed with card making. So, this challenge is sort of two-fold. First, I'm keeping in touch with people and showing them that I care via post. Second, the cards will be little projects to use the right side of my brain. 

I'll be compiling the cards here, though probably not whatever I write in them. My plan is to post once or twice a week with pictures, cards, and any replies I get.
